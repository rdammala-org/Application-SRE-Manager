Reliability Principles

01

Prevent, Don't React

The best incident is the one that never happens. Auto-detect risks, self-heal failures, eliminate recurring patterns before customers are impacted.

02

Observe Everything

Application telemetry is non-negotiable. If you can't see it, you can't prevent it. Build observability as the foundation for every operational decision.

03

Resolve Fast, Learn Faster

When incidents happen, drive resolution with decisive action. Then invest deeply in post-incident learning — every outage is a gift of knowledge.

04

Build Teams That Own Outcomes

High-performing reliability teams aren't built on process — they're built on ownership, critical thinking, and the relentless pursuit of improvement.
